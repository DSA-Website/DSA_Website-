Who We Are

Hello there! We are a student-led organization at UT Austin dedicated to fostering mutual understanding, respect, and friendship across cultures, faiths, and perspectives.

Feel free to join us in our interfaith events, cultural exchanges, and community gatherings to learn from one another, build bridges and make friends!

Our Mission

At Dialogue Student Association (DSA), our mission is to inspire and connect people by creating spaces where ideas, cultures, and experiences come together. We build bridges by fostering friendships across backgrounds and beliefs, and we celebrate our differences through cultural exchange and shared experiences.
