Past Events

Click here to view upcoming events!

General Meetings

Our general meetings are where we connect as a community, share updates, and plan upcoming events. Everyone is welcome to join, contribute ideas, and get involved.

Annual Dinner

Our annual dinner brings members together for a night of reflection, celebration, and good food. It’s a chance to look back on our accomplishments and connect over shared values.

Bake Sales

From homemade treats to fresh goodies, our bake sales on Speedway are a fun way to raise funds and share a little sweetness with the UT community.

Picnics

The DSA Nowruz picnic is a laid-back day filled with games, food, and community bonding—an opportunity to relax and enjoy nature with familiar faces while celebrating the arrival of spring!

Profit Shares

We team up with local restaurants and cafes for profit share events where a portion of proceeds support our club. Eat out, give back!

Game Night

Game Night is all about laughter, friendly competition, and bonding. Whether it’s board games, card games, or party games, it’s a laid-back chance to unwind and have fun together.

Thanksgiving Dinner

Our Thanksgiving dinner is a warm, welcoming event where we gather to share a meal and reflect on gratitude. Open to all, it’s a home away from home during the holiday season.

Iftars

During the blessed month of Ramadan, we host community iftars where students come together to break their fast, share a meal, and reflect in unity. It’s a spiritually uplifting evening open to all, rooted in generosity and togetherness.

Tea & Coffee Nights

Our tea and coffee nights are cozy, casual gatherings where members relax, sip warm drinks, and enjoy good conversation. Sometimes we team up with other student orgs to build connections and foster cross-community dialogue.
